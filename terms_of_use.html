4. The Services

Offbookly is a mobile application designed to help actors and performers rehearse their lines. Core features include:

  • Script Parsing: Users upload screenplays which are analyzed and broken down by scene, character, and dialogue using third-party AI language models.
  • Text-to-Speech: Dialogue from other characters is sent from our Cloud Run backend server to Google Cloud Text-to-Speech to generate audio, allowing users to rehearse with realistic scene partners. Your device does not communicate directly with Google TTS.
  • Practice Tools: Users can practice their lines and track their rehearsal progress.
