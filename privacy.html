OFFBOOKLY — PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: March 29, 2026

1. Introduction

Offbookly ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Offbookly mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App and related services (the "Services").

By using the Services, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Data Type What We Collect Why
Account Information Name, email address, profile photo (if provided) Account creation, authentication, communication
Authentication Data Credentials from Apple, Google, or Facebook Sign-In (OAuth tokens, unique user ID) Secure login without us storing your social media password
Your Content Screenplays, scripts, and text you upload Provide the core script-parsing and rehearsal services

2.2 Information Generated Through Your Use

Data Type What We Collect Why
Generated Audio TTS audio files created from your scripts Allow you to replay rehearsal audio across sessions
Parsed Script Data Scenes, characters, and dialogue extracted by AI Enable the rehearsal experience
Usage Data Features used, session duration, interactions Improve the App and fix bugs
Device Information Device type, OS version, app version Ensure compatibility and troubleshoot issues

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Payment card details: All payments are processed by Apple App Store (and in the future, Google Play Store). We never see or store your credit card number.
  • Precise location data: We do not collect GPS or fine-grained location data.
  • Contacts, photos, or microphone data: We do not access your device's contacts, photo library, or microphone unless a specific feature requires it and you grant explicit permission.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 To Provide the Services

  • Parse your scripts using third-party AI language models (OpenAI, Anthropic)
  • Generate rehearsal audio by sending dialogue text from our Cloud Run backend server to Google Cloud Text-to-Speech (your device does not communicate directly with Google TTS)
  • Store your account data, scripts, and audio on Firebase so you can access them across sessions

3.2 To Communicate With You

  • Send essential service notifications (account verification, subscription updates, Terms changes)
  • Respond to your support inquiries

3.3 To Improve the Services

  • Analyze aggregated, anonymized usage patterns to improve features and user experience
  • Identify and fix bugs and performance issues

3.4 To Protect Our Services

  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or violations of our Terms of Use
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do NOT use your information for: selling your personal data to third parties; targeted advertising; training or fine-tuning AI models.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Where Your Data is Stored

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers located in the United States (nam5). By using the Services, you consent to the transfer and storage of your data in this location.

5.2 How We Protect Your Data

We implement commercially reasonable security measures, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Encryption of data at rest (Firebase default encryption)
  • Authentication via secure OAuth 2.0 protocols
  • Access controls limiting employee access to user data

5.3 No Absolute Guarantee

Despite our efforts, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data. You use the Services and upload content at your own risk.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

6.1 While Your Account is Active

We retain Your Content (scripts, audio, parsed data) and account information for as long as your account is active and the Services are being provided.

6.2 Upon Account Deletion

When you delete your account:

  • Your account information, scripts, and generated audio will be removed from our active systems within 30 days
  • Backup copies may persist for up to an additional 90 days before being permanently deleted
  • Data that has already been transmitted to third-party AI providers for processing is subject to those providers' retention policies (OpenAI and Anthropic state they retain API data for up to 30 days for abuse monitoring, then delete)

6.3 Content Deletion Before Account Deletion

You may delete individual scripts or audio files at any time through the App. Deleted content will be removed from our active systems within a reasonable timeframe.

6.4 Anonymized Data

We may retain anonymized, aggregated data that cannot be used to identify you (e.g., total number of scripts processed, average session length) indefinitely for analytics purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

The Services are not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete such information.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at info@offbookly.com.
